Courses
WEB PRODUCTION

MTM6330

Despite the variety of websites found online today there are many common problems that every developer needs to solve. Building on the web design and development skills learn how to increase their productivity by using a variety of industry standard tools and frameworks.Students learn the proper use of Github and abilities it offers to improve web production. Furthermore, Sass, Gulp, Bootstrap and jquery are also taught so that students obtain easier methods of coding. All students must use these mechanisms on their final project to showcase their understanding. The final project is team based, and allows students to gain knowledge from their peers and experience the potential work flow in the field of web production.

APPLIED PROJECTS

MTM6360

Students are provided an opportunity to work with live clients, using their skills to build real-world applications and multimedia products. Working in teams, students plan, manage and develop a project though the full project cycle; from proposal to post-mortem, while developing teamwork skills and using techniques and best practices acquired through the Project Management Skills class. Students are anticipated to use Basecamp to connect with team members as well as their client. The learning objective through this site is how to stay in proper communication with each other, as well as to keep each other on task. Professors watch over students to give constructive criticism and assistance if needed.

VIDEO FOUNDATIONS

MTM6200

Students learn the full video production life cycle, from planning and pre-production, to editing and post-production. Video and audio technology tools are introduced, building on the techniques and best practices developed through DSLR photography. Students practice working through pre-production and production of both video and audio, ensuring the best quality result when transitioning to post-production to limit the amount of correcting needed during the editing process. Pre-production methods are taught to students so they are better equipt for the production process. For example, they are expected to make storyboards before filming. Students also learn the proper use of equipment such as: lighting, cameras, microphones, etc. They are also taught the many options available to them for each item.
